Chinese Braised Pork Belly with Tofu & Potato

For 5 People

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 2 hours 20 minutes | Total: ~2.5 hours

Ingredients

Main:

  • cut ginger and garlic first
  • prepare bowl of sauce
  • cut porc 1.2 kg (6-8 slices?) before blanche, cut into 4–5 cm cubes
  • (do later as put in only after 1hr) 800g potatoes (about 5–6 medium), peeled and cut into large chunks
  • (do later as put in only after potatoes) 400g firm tofu, cut into 4 cm cubes

Sauce & Aromatics:

  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing wine)
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons rock sugar
  • 4 slices ginger
  • 5 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 3–4 spring onions, cut into lengths (white and green parts separated)
  • 2 star anise (optional)
  • 800–1000 ml water

Instructions

  • Ginger/ail: cut before the rest. Then prepare bowl of sauce.
  • Blanch the Pork: Boil pork belly cubes for 5–8 minutes. Drain and rinse.
  • Caramelize Sugar: Heat 2 tbsp oil, add rock sugar and stir until amber. Add pork and brown.
  • Build Sauce: Add ginger, garlic, white spring onions. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, cooking wine, and ketchup.
  • Braise Pork: Add water to cover, star anise & cinnamon. Simmer covered for 1 hour.
  • Add Potatoes: Add potato chunks. Simmer another 30–40 minutes.
  • Add Tofu: Gently add tofu cubes. Simmer uncovered 15–20 minutes until sauce thickens.
  • Finish: Adjust seasoning, add green onions. Rest 5–10 minutes before serving.

    • Serving

    Serve hot with steamed white rice.

    Freezing Instructions

    Can be frozen for 2–3 months (best quality).

    • Cool completely
    • Portion into smaller containers
    • Remove as much air as possible (press plastic wrap on surface if using boxes)
    • Thaw overnight in fridge and reheat thoroughly

    Enjoy your meal! 🍲
