2018 dates for your agenda



Please note that different countries do not always celebrate a festival on the same date.
  • Monday 01 January: New Year's Day

  • Friday 26 January: Australia Day (fixed)

  • Wednesday 31 January: Thaipusam

  • Sunday 04 February: Super Bowl LII at U.S. Bank Stadium in Minneapolis

  • Friday 09 February: 2018 Winter Olympics in Pyeongchang, South Korea (ends on 25 February)

  • Wednesday 14 February: Valentine's Day

  • Friday 16 February: Chinese New Year (Year of the Dog)

  • Wednesday 28 February: There are 28 days in February 2018

  • Saturday 17 March: St. Patrick's Day (fixed)

  • Sunday 25 March: Europe changes to summer time (02h00 becomes 03h00)

  • Friday 30 March: Good Friday

  • Sunday 01 April: Easter Sunday

  • Sunday 01 April: April Fool's Day

  • Monday 02 April: Easter Monday

  • Wednesday 04 April: XXI Commonwealth Games in Gold Coast City, Queensland, Australia (ends on 15 April)

  • Monday 23 April: St. George's Day (England's national day) (fixed)

  • Tuesday 01 May: May Day (International Workers' Day)

  • Sunday 13 May: Mother's Day (2nd Sunday of May in most countries but France on Sunday 27 May). Check your country.

  • Tuesday 29 May: Vesak Day

  • Saturday 02 June: Republic Day of Italy (fixed)

  • Tuesday 12 June: National Day of Russia (fixed)

  • Thursday 14 June: 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia (ends on 15 July)

  • Friday 15 June: Hari Raya Puasa

  • Sunday 17 June: Father's Day (3rd Sunday of June in most countries). Check your country.

  • Sunday 01 July: Canada Day (fixed)

  • Wednesday 04 July: Independence Day USA (fixed)

  • Saturday 14 July: French National Day (Le quatorze juillet) (fixed)

  • Thursday 02 August: European Championships (inaugural) in Glasgow and Berlin (ends on 12 August)

  • Thursday 09 August: Singapore's National Day (fixed)

  • Wednesday 15 August: India's Independence Day (fixed)

  • Wednesday 22 August: Hari Raya Haji

  • Sunday 16 September: Malaysia Day (fixed)

  • Monday 01 October: National Day of the People's Republic of China (fixed)

  • Sunday 28 October: Europe changes to winter time (03h00 becomes 02h00)

  • Wednesday 31 October: Halloween Day (fixed)

  • Thursday 01 November: All Saints' Day (fixed)

  • Tuesday 06 November: Deepavali

  • Tuesday 25 December: Christmas Day (fixed)
