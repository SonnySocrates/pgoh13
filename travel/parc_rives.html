What's New in Paris


Parc Rives de Seine (officially opened on 2 April 2017)
Put mouse on photo to stop the slide display.

Paris City Hall proudly claims a new 4.5km promenade along the right bank of the Seine River starting from Les Tuileries and ending at La Bastille. Called "Parc Rives de Seine". It is meant to join up with the 2.5km promenade along the left bank to total 7km in all. The opening ceremony by Anne Hidalgo, the Mayor of Paris, was held on April 2, 2017. However much of it is not new, as the heavily-cobbled surfaces remain unchanged, making it difficult for a promenade if one is not wearing comfortable sneakers. The renovative part really starts from the Pont Neuf side and ends at Pont Marie near the Notre-Dame Cathedral. In fact on a sunny day you should start your walk from the Pont Neuf side and not the Notre-Dame side if you do not want to have the sun's rays smack in your eyes!
