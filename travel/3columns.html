first column For sauce to pour over pomfret steamed fish: can use capitain (Paristore) or tilapia (African market) Secret of Chinese cooking is to have everything ready before starting to cook. Put big piece of ginger on plate. Rub salt and pepper on fish, then put it on plate so just remove fish when steamed. Leave for 15 minutes to be sure. For the sauce I experimented with the following: Preparation: (a) Put in a bowl equal amounts of both thin and thick soja sauce (1/2 of bowl, I put a lot as I need the sauce for the accompanying rice), about the same amount of rice wine, one coffee packet of sugar and stir. (b) Cut into thin slices: ginger and spring onion (or half an onion). Hash some 3-4 garlic. To put all together into pan later. When pan is hot put in thin layer of groundnut oil in small pan and when oil smokes put in some thinly sliced ginger, thinly-sliced spring onion (or half onion), minced garlic and pour the bowl mixture in the pan. Turn off the heat, add some sesame oil to the pan before removing it almost immediately to pour over the fish.
