  VISITING BARCELONA (4)  


"The Head" by American pop artist Roy Lichtenstein for the 1992 Barcelona Olympics, stands prominently in Port Vell. The "Catedral de Barcelona" is right in the middle of the Gothic quarter.

The house where Gaudi lived for 20 years (from 1906 until his death in 1926). A simple Spanish menu. The starter is often quite heavy - it can be a plate of fideos (spaghetti) or even paella.

The esplanade along the Barceloneta beach is broad enough to accommodate rollerbladers, joggers and holiday-makers. After a swim in the sea at the Barceloneta beach, you can shower yourself for free at such water points.

Day trips to Montserrat and Sitges.
