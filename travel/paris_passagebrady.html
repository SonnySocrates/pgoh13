Indian restaurants at Passage Brady, Paris

I personally find that this exotic passage is too tourist-orientated. As a result you don't get authentic Indian curry. And not only that - your pocket suffers too as the prices are also tourist-oriented! Go rather to the restaurants around Rue du Faubourg St. Denis near the La Chapelle underground station where you will get authentic Indian curries at very reasonable prices as they cater mainly to the local Indian clientele.
