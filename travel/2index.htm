For armchair travellers

You can either take unnecessary risks of being quarantined in a foreign country by travelling during this period of pandemic or you can do it in the safety of your own home by being an armchair traveller like me. If you like to join in, well be my guest! Some of the articles are quite old, by the way, but I hope they will still be of interest to someone out there! But make sure you are on WiFi as the big number of photos consume lots of megabytes! Just click on the name of the city.
