Little India at La Chapelle, Paris

When you exit La Chapelle tube station turn left for Rue du Faubourg St. Denis (in the direction of Gare du Nord) and you will find yourself among a multitude of Indians, Sri Lankans and Pakistanis frequenting the many Cash and Carry grocery shops and Indian restaurants there. To be more exact, this area can be called Little Jaffna, as most of them are Tamils from Sri Lanka. Don't get off at the wrong station. La Chapelle is not the same station as the one at Porte de la Chapelle.
