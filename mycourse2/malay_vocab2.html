Enlarging your Malay vocabulary (List 2)

If you are really keen to reach a high level of Malay try to get hold of a good Malay-English dictionary in order to be able to understand what you read in the Malay newspapers.

WORDS AND EXPRESSIONS:
di kediamannya = at his house (kediaman = house, residence)
keizinan = permission, approval
ahli-ahli keluarganya = his family members
mendiamkan diri = kept quiet
khususnya = especially
malangnya = unfortunately
ditangguhkan = postponed
membuat keputusan = to make a decision
jatuh cinta dengan = to fall in love with
kad pengenalan = identity card
hadiah = gift, prize
layak = qualified
iklan = advertisement
membekalkan = to supply, provide
denda = a fine, money paid as a penalty
warganegara Malaysia = Malaysian citizen
raptai = a rehearsal

SENTENCES:
Beliau berkata, majlis perkahwinan akan dilangsungkan secara kecil-kecilan. = He said that the wedding ceremony would be held on a small scale.
Kerajaan telah beberapa kali mengingatkan orang ramai supaya menukar kad pengenalan lama kepada MyKad. = The Government had on a number of occasions reminded the public to change their old identity cards to MyKad.
Ahli-ahli perniagaan dan pelabur Amerika boleh menggunakan Malaysia sebagai batu loncatan untuk membuat pelaburan di negara-negara Islam, kata Perdana Menteri, Datuk Seri Abdullah Ahmad Badawi. = American businessmen and investors can use Malaysia as a stepping stone for investments in Islamic countries, said Prime Minister Datuk Seri Abdullah Ahmad Badawi.

Are you ready to tackle a whole paragraph? Here goes:
Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad hari ini menjelaskan beliau memulangkan tujuh kereta Proton kepada pihak pengurusan syarikat itu setelah selesai memandu uji semua kenderaan berkenaan. "Kereta-kereta itu dihantar kepada saya untuk dipandu uji tanpa disertakan dengan dokumentasi yang rasmi. Setelah diuji, saya pulangkan semula kepada mereka. Kereta ini milik Proton, bukan milik saya. Saya pun tidak memerlukan terlalu banyak kereta," kata bekas Perdana Menteri.

Translation:
Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad today explained (clarified) that he returned seven Proton cars to the company management after having finished a test drive on all the specified cars. "The cars were sent to me for testing but they were not accompanied by any official documentation. After having tested them, I returned the cars to them. These cars belong to Proton, they're not mine. Besides I don't need too many cars," said the former Prime Minister.
