Dishes with Ch cooking wine, oyster sauce, ketchup & cornstarch (5 people)

💡 Quick Tips for All Recipes

  • Marinate meats with soy sauce + cooking wine.
  • Use cornstarch slurry to thicken sauces for glossy finish.


Sweet & Sour Chicken for 5 People

Ingredients:

  • 800g boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 bell peppers (red & green), cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
Sauce:
  • 6 tbsp ketchup
  • 4 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp vinegar
  • Garlic & ginger

Method: Marinate chicken, fry until crispy, stir-fry veggies, combine with thickened sauce. Serve with rice.

Beef with Oyster Sauce for 5 People

  • 700g beef (sirloin or flank), thinly sliced
  • 500g broccoli or mixed vegetables
Marinade: 2 tbsp soy sauce + 2 tbsp cooking wine + 2 tsp cornstarch
Sauce: 4 tbsp oyster sauce + 2 tbsp soy sauce + 2 tbsp cooking wine + 2 tsp ketchup

Quick stir-fry beef, then vegetables, combine with sauce.

General Tso’s Chicken for 5 People

  • 800g chicken, cut into tiny pieces
  • Bell peppers and onions as needed
Sauce:
  • 5 tbsp ketchup
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
  • Chili, garlic, sugar

Fry chicken and coat in glossy sauce. Add sesame seeds.

Stir-Fried Vegetables for 5 People

  • 1.2kg mixed vegetables (broccoli, bok choy, carrots, snap peas)
Sauce: 4 tbsp oyster sauce + 3 tbsp soy sauce + 2 tbsp cooking wine + 2 tsp ketchup + garlic & ginger

High-heat stir-fry until crisp-tender.

Chinese-Style Braised Pork or Chicken Wings for 5 People

  • 1.2kg pork belly/shoulder OR 1.5kg chicken wings
Sauce:
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 4 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
  • 4 tbsp ketchup
  • Garlic, ginger, sugar

Brown meat, simmer in sauce with water until tender and glazed.

Kung Pao Chicken for 5 People

  • 700g chicken, cut into tiny pieces
  • 150g cashews or peanuts
  • Vegetables and dried chilies
Sauce base: Oyster sauce + soy sauce + cooking wine + ketchup + chili

Stir-fry chicken, add veggies & nuts, coat with sauce.
